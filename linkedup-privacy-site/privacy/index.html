Who we are

LinkedUp Job Search Tool is operated by its creator. For questions or requests, email admin@alexjohnboyle.com.

Scope

This policy applies to your use of LinkedUp within the OpenAI platform and any related websites where we host legal information (e.g., this page at alexjohnboyle.com/privacy).

Your usage of OpenAI’s platform is also governed by OpenAI’s own terms and privacy policy. See: OpenAI Privacy Policy.

Data we collect

  • Conversation content you provide (e.g., prompts, job preferences, résumés) to generate responses. Where possible, we avoid storing conversation content outside OpenAI unless you opt into features that require it (e.g., saved settings, digests).
  • Usage data such as timestamps, feature usage, and basic analytics (if enabled) to understand reliability and improve the Service.
  • Contact information (e.g., email) if you reach out for support or request follow‑ups.
  • Automation metadata if you request periodic digests or similar features (e.g., schedule, preferences).
  • Third‑party content you direct the tool to access (e.g., job postings). We process only as needed to fulfill your request.

How we use data

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • Personalize results and digests according to your stated preferences.
  • Communicate with you about updates or support requests.
  • Detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

GDPR legal bases

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): for optional features like saved settings or digests.
  • Contract (Article 6(1)(b)): to deliver the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): to keep our Service reliable and secure.
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): where required by law.

How we share information

  • Processors: We rely on service providers to run the Service (e.g., OpenAI to process conversations; Cloudflare and GitHub for hosting). They act under contractual restrictions and do not acquire ownership of your data.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • No sale: We do not sell your personal information. We also do not “share” personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to deliver the Service, resolve issues, meet legal obligations, or as otherwise described here. If you request deletion, we will honor it where required and technically feasible. Note that OpenAI may retain conversation logs per its own policies.

Security

We use industry‑standard safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data we process. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we work to continuously improve protections.

Your rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, opt out of certain processing, or limit use/sensitive data. To exercise rights, email admin@alexjohnboyle.com. You will not be discriminated against for making a privacy request.

International data transfers

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your home country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for transfers.

Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the Service or applicable laws. We will update the “Effective date” and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
